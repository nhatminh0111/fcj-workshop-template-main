# “CLOUD ARCHITECT” Reflection Report

### Purpose of the Event

- Sharing knowledge through the program’s questions.
- Networking and competing between 2 teams with content related to Cloud and architecture design knowledge.

### Highlighted Content

#### Creating a playground and a method of sharing knowledge through competition between teams

- The two teams compete against each other, taking turns answering the questions in each question set, ranging from easy to difficult.
- The team that earns more points will advance to the next round.
- If both teams still have the same score at the end, there will be an 11th question, and the team that answers faster will win.
- The questions will be related to Cloud and architecture design knowledge.

### Experience During the Event

Participating in the **“CLOUD ARCHITECT”** event was a very interesting experience, helping me approach knowledge in a more accessible way. Some highlighted experiences:

#### Learning from the admins

- For highly difficult questions, the admins provided explanations, suggested appropriate solutions, and explained why those solutions were the most optimal in those situations.

#### Practical technical experience

- As one of the 8 teams participating in the event’s competition, our team felt excited to take part in this event because we were able to learn new knowledge and receive gifts through the program.

#### Lessons learned

- Clearly understanding the concepts and functions of each service is very important. From there, we can determine in which situations an AWS service can be applied effectively and how to handle errors encountered in a real-world working environment.
