### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working environment**  
The working environment at FCJ is very friendly and open. The team members are always ready to support me when I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which helps me focus better.

**2. Support from mentor / team admin**  
My mentor provided very detailed guidance, explained clearly the parts I did not understand, and always encouraged me to ask questions proactively. The admin team was also very enthusiastic in supporting administrative procedures and documents, making it easier for me to complete my work. I especially appreciated that the mentor allowed me to try and handle problems myself before giving the answer, which helped me develop independent thinking.

**3. Relevance between work and my academic background**  
The assigned tasks were quite suitable for the knowledge I had learned at university, while also expanding into new areas I had not previously approached. As a result, I strengthened my foundation while also gaining opportunities to learn practical skills.

**4. Opportunities to learn and develop skills**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, working in a team, and communicating professionally in a corporate environment. My mentor also shared many practical experiences, helping me have a clearer direction for my future work.

**5. Culture and team spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects one another, works seriously, and still maintains a pleasant atmosphere. When there is an urgent project, the whole team works together and supports one another regardless of position. This made me feel that I was truly part of the team, even though I was only an intern.

**6. Policies / benefits for interns**  
The company provided convenient conditions, including flexible working hours when necessary.

### Some other questions
- What are you most satisfied with during your internship?  
  + What I am most satisfied with is the friendly working environment, especially the admin team members who were always ready to support me whenever I had difficulties. There were times outside working hours when they still took the time to review and give detailed feedback on the AWS architecture of our group.
- If you were to recommend it to your friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?  
  + If I had friends looking for an internship, I would recommend this place because the working environment is professional yet friendly, the mentor is dedicated, and the company always creates conditions for interns to learn and develop themselves.
